Knowledge Tome - Your last Magic RSS feed

Knowledge Tome is an RSS Feed which aggregates multiple news sites about Magic Judge's world. It scrapes the following channels looking for new articles. It parses them and detects the language, so you will stop to receive uncomprehensible articles that are not in your speaking language.

The aim is to help judges around the world to stay updated. Stop checking dozens of sites weekly!

This is still a Proof of Concept, and it is open source.

If you want to report a new site or submit feedbacks, please open an issue or contact me on JudgeApps.
